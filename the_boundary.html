The Boundary

Seven cases. Five questions each. One framework under pressure. Apply Kymlicka's multicultural citizenship categories and Benhabib's normative conditions to real-world cultural rights conflicts — then bring your results to Wednesday's workshop.

Before You Begin

Read Kymlicka Chapter 8 and the Benhabib excerpts before completing this simulation. For each case you will classify the group, identify the type of right claimed, determine whether the policy involves external protection or internal restriction, check Benhabib's three conditions, and assess the adequacy of Kymlicka's framework.

There are no correct answers — the point is to test where the theory holds and where it breaks. Your responses will be aggregated and displayed during the workshop.

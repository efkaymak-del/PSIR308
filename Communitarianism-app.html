Community Self-Mapper

PSIR 308 · Communitarianism · Kymlicka Chapter 6

You will face eight scenarios involving a tension between individual autonomy and communal belonging. For each, choose the position closest to your instinct:

Liberal: Individual choice and autonomy take priority.

Communitarian: Community identity and shared values take priority.

Compromise: Both matter — individual freedom is real but situated in community.

There are no right answers. The pattern reveals your theoretical instincts.
